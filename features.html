The Bluehost Platform

The Blueprint is designed to help beginners get started with WordPress. Yet it also serves as an invaluable resource once a website has been launched. Continue to reference The Blueprint as much as you need while your website continues to grow and adapt to the ever-changing ecosystem of the internet.

Dedicated hosting is a website hosting environment that provides the highest level of resource allocation, privacy, and control. Dedicated servers are completely isolated from one another, so users get full access to configure their server.

Our technology identifies websites using excessive resources and temporarily re-assigns them to isolated systems. This defends your website performance.

Enable advanced control of ports and further protect your account by securing an IP address assigned to your websites only.

CloudFlare is built directly into your account. An advanced content delivery network, it automatically increases the performance of your site.

Simply log in once and effortlessly manage all of your shared web hosting services in one place.

CodeGuard works behind the scenes, taking daily backups and saving each as a separate version.
